# How to Use Hands-Free CV Generator

Follow these steps to create your professional CV in minutes.

## 1. Manual Entry
The **Editor** panel on the left is your main workspace.
- **Personal Info**: Fill in your basic details. Click the profile icon to upload a photo.
- **Experience & Education**: Click **"+ Add"** to create new entries. Fill in the company, position, and dates.
- **Skills & Hobbies**: Type your skill or hobby in the input box and press **Enter**.

## 2. Using AI Assistance ✨
Look for the **Sparkles** icon throughout the editor:
- **AI Generate**: In the Summary section, click this to get a professional bio.
- **AI Improve**: In the Experience section, type a basic description and click this to make it sound more professional.
- **Suggest Skills**: Click this in the Skills section to get recommendations based on your job title.

## 3. Voice Commands 🎙️
Click the **"Voice Control"** button in the header. Once it says "Listening...", you can use these commands:
- *"Set name to [Your Name]"*
- *"Set email to [Your Email]"*
- *"Set phone to [Your Phone Number]"*
- *"Set location to [Your City]"*
- *"Add skill [Skill Name]"*
- *"Add hobby [Hobby Name]"*
- *"Set summary to [Your Professional Summary]"*

## 4. Customizing the Look
- **Templates**: Use the dropdown in the header to switch between **Modern**, **Classic**, **Minimal**, and **Creative** templates.
- **Theme**: Click the **Sun/Moon** icon to toggle between Light and Dark modes.

## 5. Saving and Printing 🖨️
- **Real-time Save**: Your data is automatically saved to your browser's local storage.
- **Print / PDF**: Click the **"Print / PDF"** button.
- **Pro Tip**: If the print button doesn't work in the preview window, click the **External Link icon (↗️)** next to it to open the app in a new tab, then print from there.
- **PDF Settings**: In the print dialog, select **"Save as PDF"** and ensure **"Background Graphics"** is checked for the best results.
